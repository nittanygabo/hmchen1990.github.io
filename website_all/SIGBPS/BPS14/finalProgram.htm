Oral Presentation Guidllines:

  1. Please try to arrive at the session room for your presentation 10 minutes prior to the the session starting.
  2. Please upload your PowerPoint file onto the presentation computer and make sure that your file runs appropriately.
  3. Please introduce yourself to your session chair so that the session chair will know who is presenting the paper.
  4. Each oral presentation session is allocated 1 hour and 15 minutes for three paper presentations. Please make sure that you finish your presentation in 20 minutes and leave 5 minutes for questions. Time management is VERY critical because people may switch between sessions for presentations that they are interested in.
