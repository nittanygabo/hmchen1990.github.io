SIGBPS Track: Mobile Web and Services

Track Chairs:

Dongsong Zhang (University of Maryland, Baltimore County)
Lina Zhou (University of Maryland, Baltimore County)

Track Description:

The rapid advances in ubiquitous technologies make fruition of more than two decades of mobile computing. The advent of web services and the progress on mobile handheld devices, ambient components, user-generated content, mobile communications, and new business models have propelled many new mobile applications and services. Web services technologies along with their integration into mobility and new business models provide a technical infrastructure that enables the progress of mobile services and applications. These include dynamic and on-demand mobile services, context-aware mobile services, and mobile web services.

The Mobile Web and Services track of SIGPAM solicits novel research and industrial contributions to the field of mobile computing and mobile services. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Challenges in mobile environments
  • Context-aware mobile services
  • Design, implementation, and evaluation of mobile systems and applications
  • Knowledge and service discovery in mobile environments
  • Mobile Web access and adaptation
  • Mobile gaming
  • Mobile HCI
  • Mobile healthcare services
  • Mobile learning and education
  • Mobile search and advertising
  • Mobile Web services
  • On-demand mobile services
  • Software architecture for mobile applications
